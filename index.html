My (Y)IEPI Application

The purpose of this application is to give evaluators a digital possibility of browsing my provided documention to the program.

With an interactive way of navigating from Job records, Academia and exploring the locations in the map which are relevant to my career, I aim at offering a cooler way of being evaluated.

Having the pin provided with the CV in hands, the evaluator can start the exciting experience of knowing more about me.
